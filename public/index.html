Emergency Pro Tips
  • Remove water by mopping and blotting
  • Speed drying by opening drawers and cabinets
  • Remove wet area rugs and other non-fixed floor coverings
  • Wipe down furniture, dry cushions and place aluminum foil between furniture legs and floor coverings
  • Lift draperies off the floor and secure in higher area
  • Move art, photos and the like to dry areas

Prevent Further Damage

Don't make these common mistakes

Leave it to the PROS
  • Turn off electricity in affected rooms
  • Don't dry with an household vacuum
  • Don't remove fixed flood coverings
  • Don't use appliances in affected rooms
  • Don't disturb visible mold
  • Don't open books or remove them from shelves
